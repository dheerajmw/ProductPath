# ProductPath — Problem Statement

## The Problem

Product hiring still leans heavily on resumes, degrees, university brands, and past employers—not on whether someone can actually do the job.

Aspiring product professionals often spend months in courses, fellowships, bootcamps, and self-study, yet struggle to show what they can do in a structured, credible way.

Recruiters face the mirror image of that gap: large applicant pools with little signal on real product thinking, execution, and practical skills. Traditional channels rarely surface capability clearly.

### Consequences

| Stakeholder | Impact |
|-------------|--------|
| **Candidates** | Skilled people stay invisible; effort in learning does not translate into trust with employers. |
| **Recruiters** | Excessive time spent screening; weak confidence before interviews. |
| **Learning platforms** | Education and employment stay disconnected. |
| **The market** | Hiring stays credential-driven instead of capability-driven. |

## Our Approach

ProductPath is a **skill-first talent network**. Candidates learn, get assessed, prove ability through projects, earn verification, and become discoverable to hiring partners based on **demonstrated skills**—not pedigree alone.

---

## Vision

Build the most trusted network for product talent, where opportunity is earned through demonstrated capability—not educational background, university reputation, or prior company logos.

---

## Core Principles

### Skills over credentials

Hiring and discovery should reflect demonstrated skills and execution, not proxies like school or employer name.

### Verification through proof

Trust comes from assessments and reviewed work—not self-reported claims on a profile.

### Continuous relevance

Profiles stay current through periodic reassessment and refreshed proof of work.

### Career progression

The product guides users from learning toward hiring readiness in clear, actionable steps.

### Trust for recruiters

Recruiters can quickly and confidently identify interview-ready talent with evidence behind every signal.
